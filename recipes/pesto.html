Pesto Pasta

Home

This supper couldn't be easier. Make our filling pesto pasta recipe for a quick, family-friendly dish you can throw together with storecupboard ingredients

This is very quick and takes about 10 minutes to cook & can serve 6 people

Ingredints

  • 500g pack spaghetti , fresh or dried
  • 2-3tbsp pesto from a jar
  • 50g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), shaved or grated

Method

  1. Boil spaghetti in a large pan according to pack instructions
  2. Drain, then return to the pan with the pesto and half the parmesan. Toss & then serve
  3. Add the remaining Parmesan
